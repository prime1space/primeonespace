Our Story

Empowering Vavuniya's Entrepreneurial Spirit

Prime One Coworking Space was founded with a vision to transform how professionals work in Vavuniya. We provide more than just a desk – we offer a thriving community, world-class amenities, and the infrastructure needed to succeed in today's digital economy.

Our Mission

To provide Vavuniya's professionals, entrepreneurs, and businesses with a premium workspace ecosystem that fosters productivity, collaboration, and growth. We're committed to breaking geographical barriers with world-class connectivity and amenities.

Our Vision

To become Sri Lanka's premier regional coworking hub, bridging the gap between urban and rural entrepreneurship. We envision a future where location is no barrier to success, and every professional has access to world-class infrastructure.
