# Refund and Cancellation Policy

**Last Updated**: February 16, 2026  
**Effective Date**: February 16, 2026

## 1. Overview

This Refund and Cancellation Policy outlines the terms and conditions for canceling bookings and requesting refunds at PrimeOne Coworking Space.

## 2. Cancellation Policy

### 2.1 Hourly Bookings

**More than 24 hours before booking**:
- ✅ Full refund (100%)
- ✅ No cancellation fee
- ✅ Instant processing

**12-24 hours before booking**:
- ✅ 50% refund
- ⚠️ 50% cancellation fee
- ✅ Processed within 5-7 business days

**Less than 12 hours before booking**:
- ❌ No refund
- ⚠️ 100% cancellation fee
- ℹ️ Booking amount forfeited

**No-show** (failure to arrive):
- ❌ No refund
- ❌ No rescheduling
- ℹ️ Full amount forfeited

### 2.2 Daily Bookings

**More than 48 hours before booking**:
- ✅ Full refund (100%)
- ✅ No cancellation fee

**24-48 hours before booking**:
- ✅ 50% refund
- ⚠️ 50% cancellation fee

**Less than 24 hours before booking**:
- ❌ No refund
- ⚠️ 100% cancellation fee

### 2.3 Monthly Memberships

**Before membership start date**:
- ✅ Full refund (100%)
- ✅ No cancellation fee

**Within first 7 days of membership**:
- ✅ Pro-rated refund
- ⚠️ Minus one week's fee

**After 7 days**:
- ❌ No refund for current month
- ✅ Can cancel for next month (30 days notice required)

### 2.4 Event Registrations

**More than 7 days before event**:
- ✅ Full refund (100%)
- ✅ No cancellation fee

**3-7 days before event**:
- ✅ 50% refund
- ⚠️ 50% cancellation fee

**Less than 3 days before event**:
- ❌ No refund
- ⚠️ 100% cancellation fee

## 3. How to Cancel

### 3.1 Online Cancellation
1. Log in to your account
2. Go to "My Bookings"
3. Select the booking to cancel
4. Click "Cancel Booking"
5. Confirm cancellation
6. Receive confirmation email

### 3.2 Email Cancellation
- Send email to: bookings@primeone.space
- Include: Booking ID, Name, Email, Reason
- Response within 24 hours

### 3.3 Phone Cancellation
- Call: +94 70 623 3612
- Available: Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM
- Provide booking details

## 4. Refund Processing

### 4.1 Refund Timeline
- **Credit/Debit Card**: 5-7 business days
- **Bank Transfer**: 7-10 business days
- **Digital Wallets**: 3-5 business days

### 4.2 Refund Method
- Refunds issued to original payment method
- Cannot refund to different account
- Processing fees may apply (bank-dependent)

### 4.3 Refund Confirmation
- Email confirmation sent when refund processed
- Check your bank statement after timeline
- Contact us if refund not received

## 5. Modifications and Rescheduling

### 5.1 Modification Policy

**More than 24 hours before booking**:
- ✅ Free modification (one time)
- ✅ Subject to availability
- ⚠️ Price difference may apply

**Less than 24 hours before booking**:
- ⚠️ Modification fee: 25% of booking value
- ✅ Subject to availability
- ⚠️ Price difference may apply

### 5.2 How to Modify
1. Cancel existing booking (per cancellation policy)
2. Create new booking
3. Or contact us for assistance

## 6. Exceptions and Special Circumstances

### 6.1 Force Majeure
Full refund provided for cancellations due to:
- Natural disasters
- Government restrictions
- Public health emergencies
- Facility closures

### 6.2 Facility Issues
Full refund if we cancel due to:
- Maintenance issues
- Overbooking
- Facility unavailability
- Safety concerns

### 6.3 Medical Emergencies
- Provide medical certificate
- Full refund considered on case-by-case basis
- Contact us within 48 hours

## 7. Non-Refundable Items

The following are **NOT refundable**:
- ❌ Booking fees and service charges
- ❌ Add-on services already consumed
- ❌ Promotional discounts (if booking cancelled)
- ❌ No-show bookings
- ❌ Late cancellations (as per policy)

## 8. Refund Disputes

### 8.1 Dispute Process
1. Contact us within 14 days of issue
2. Provide booking details and evidence
3. We review within 5 business days
4. Decision communicated via email

### 8.2 Escalation
If unsatisfied with resolution:
- Email: disputes@primeone.space
- Include all previous correspondence
- Senior management review
- Final decision within 10 business days

## 9. Chargebacks

### 9.1 Before Filing Chargeback
- Contact us first to resolve
- Most issues resolved within 48 hours
- Chargebacks may result in account suspension

### 9.2 Chargeback Consequences
- Account may be suspended
- Future bookings may be restricted
- Legal action may be taken for fraudulent chargebacks
