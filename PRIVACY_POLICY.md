# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: February 16, 2026  
**Effective Date**: February 16, 2026

## Introduction

PrimeOne Coworking Space ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Name, email address, phone number, password
- **Profile Information**: Country, address, date of birth, gender, emergency contact
- **Booking Information**: Workspace preferences, dates, times, seat selections
- **Payment Information**: Payment method details (processed securely)
- **Communications**: Messages, support requests, feedback

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data**: Pages visited, features used, time spent
- **Device Information**: IP address, browser type, operating system
- **Location Data**: General location based on IP address
- **Cookies**: Session cookies, preference cookies, analytics cookies

### 1.3 Information from Third Parties
- **Payment Processors**: Transaction confirmations
- **Social Media**: If you connect social accounts (optional)

## 2. How We Use Your Information

### 2.1 To Provide Services
- Create and manage your account
- Process bookings and payments
- Send booking confirmations and receipts
- Manage event registrations
- Provide customer support

### 2.2 To Improve Services
- Analyze usage patterns
- Develop new features
- Conduct research and analytics
- Optimize user experience

### 2.3 To Communicate
- Send service updates and notifications
- Respond to inquiries
- Send marketing communications (with consent)
- Send security alerts

### 2.4 For Security
- Prevent fraud and abuse
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- Comply with legal obligations
- Protect our rights and property

## 3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your data based on:
- **Contract**: To fulfill our agreement with you
- **Consent**: When you opt-in to marketing
- **Legitimate Interests**: To improve services and prevent fraud
- **Legal Obligation**: To comply with laws

## 4. How We Share Your Information

### 4.1 We DO Share With:
- **Service Providers**: Payment processors, email services, hosting providers
- **Legal Authorities**: When required by law or to protect rights
- **Business Transfers**: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale

### 4.2 We DO NOT:
- Sell your personal data to third parties
- Share your data for third-party marketing
- Publicly disclose your personal information

## 5. Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: We retain data while your account is active
- **Inactive Accounts**: Data deleted after 2 years of inactivity
- **Booking Records**: Retained for 7 years for tax/legal purposes
- **Marketing Data**: Until you unsubscribe or withdraw consent

## 6. Your Rights

### 6.1 Access and Portability
- Request a copy of your data
- Download your data in machine-readable format

### 6.2 Correction and Deletion
- Update incorrect information
- Request deletion of your account and data

### 6.3 Restriction and Objection
- Restrict processing of your data
- Object to processing for marketing purposes

### 6.4 Withdraw Consent
- Unsubscribe from marketing emails
- Disable cookies in browser settings

### 6.5 Lodge a Complaint
- Contact your data protection authority

**To exercise your rights, email**: privacy@primeone.space

## 7. Data Security

### 7.1 Security Measures
- Encryption of data in transit (SSL/TLS)
- Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and authentication
- Rate limiting to prevent attacks

### 7.2 Your Responsibility
- Keep your password secure
- Use strong, unique passwords
- Log out from shared devices
- Report suspicious activity

## 8. International Data Transfers

- Your data may be transferred to servers outside your country
- We ensure adequate protection through:
  - Standard contractual clauses

  - Your explicit consent

## 9. Children's Privacy

- If we discover such data, we will delete it promptly
